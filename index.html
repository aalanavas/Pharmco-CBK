Pharmacy Closure Crisis

The NCPA stated that nearly one pharmacy closed every day in the U.S. in 2023.

15.8 million people in the U.S. live in areas identified as pharmacy deserts, lacking adequate access to pharmacies. 94.5% of pharmacy deserts had no pharmacies located within them at all.
